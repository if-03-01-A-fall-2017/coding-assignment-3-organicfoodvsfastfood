Organic food labels

Organic food labels in Austria

In Austria, four quality labels are accepted:

AMA seal
Products with excellent quality, traceable origin and independent controls.
AMA organic label
Organic food with certified quality and traceable origin.
Austria quality mark
Products, services and food that guarantee local quality.
ÖGE quality mark
Nutrition-optimized food quality in the public catering.

Organic food labels in Europa

EU organic seal
Biologically produced foods that are produced according to the directives of the EU regulations
Protected designation of origin
Foods where all production steps take place in a defined area
Protected Geographical Indication
Foods where at least one of the production steps takes place in a defined area
Guaranteed traditional specialty
Product of traditional composition or traditional manufacturing and / or processing method
